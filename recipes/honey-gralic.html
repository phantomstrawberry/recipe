Honey Garlic Glazed Salmon

A 15-minute dinner that tastes like you actually tried.

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 3 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)

steps

  1. Sear: Season salmon with salt and pepper. In a pan over medium-high heat, sear the salmon skin-side up for about 4 minutes until golden.
  2. Flip: Turn the fillets over.
  3. Glaze: Whisk the honey, soy sauce, lemon, and garlic together. Pour it into the pan.
  4. Baste: Let the sauce bubble and thicken for 2-3 minutes while spooning it over the fish until cooked through.
