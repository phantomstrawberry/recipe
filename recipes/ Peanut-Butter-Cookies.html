Peanut Butter Cookies

For when your sweet tooth is screaming but your pantry is bare

Ingredients

  • 1 cup peanut butter (creamy works best)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 large egg

steps

  1. Mix: Preheat your oven to 175°C (350°F). Stir all three ingredients together in a bowl until smooth.
  2. Scoop: Roll into small balls (about 1 inch) and place on a baking sheet.
  3. Cross-hatch: Use a fork to press a criss-cross pattern into the top of each ball (this flattens them).
  4. Bake: Bake for 10-12 minutes. Let them cool completely—they'll be fragile while hot but will firm up into chewy perfection.
