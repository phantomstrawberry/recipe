Classic Shakshuka

The ultimate "eggs for dinner" (or breakfast) move

Ingredients

  • 1 onion (diced)
  • 1 red bell pepper (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp cumin + 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 5-6 eggs
  • Fresh parsley or feta (optional)

steps

  1. Sauté: Cook the onion and pepper in a large skillet until soft. Add garlic and spices; cook for 1 minute.
  2. Simmer: Pour in the tomatoes. Let it simmer for 10 minutes until it thickens slightly.
  3. Crack: Use a spoon to make small wells in the sauce. Crack an egg into each well.
  4. Cover: Put a lid on the pan and cook for 5-8 minutes until the whites are set but the yolks are still runny. Top with feta or parsley
